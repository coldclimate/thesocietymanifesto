Who's behind this?

My name is Oli Wood and I work in IT in the UK. I've never been a member of a political party, I guess I identify as being left of centre and until now I've done nothing to try and change the country. In October 2020 I got too frustrated with the UK government and political system to sit on my hands so I made a document I could share that was an early draft of The Society Manifesto. In 2022 I finally got round to buying a domain name and building this little website.

Are you collecting my data?

As little as possible. There are no analytics packages in this page (Google Analytics and the like) and it loads no JavaScript or content from other domains. I do get numbers of page loads and country level geography information because of how I host it, but that's it.

What can I do to help?

The source for the site is available, contributions welcomed by Pull Request.

If you agree with The Society Manifesto then please share it with folks you know, maybe even your MP using the excellent Write To Them site.

If you're an MP and you want to pledge to follow The Society Manifesto that would be great, link to it in your public materials.

How do I get in touch?

Please don't. I'm a pretty private person and I don't want to talk about The Society Manifesto publicly on TV or whatever. By all means talk about it, you don't need me
